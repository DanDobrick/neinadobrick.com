Repayment Plan

90k word completed manuscript

New veterinarian Ria Butler is drowning in student loans. She's done the math. A full time job isn't going to cut it. So when the chance to make them disappear falls into her lap, Ria doesn't ask too many questions. She trades in her cozy, San Francisco apartment for a remote ranch in Montana…and immediately regrets her life choices.

A tractor nearly took her head off this morning. The ladder in the hayloft keeps disappearing — while she's still in the hayloft — and her new boss is convinced Ria is just a useless city girl. She thinks his opinions blow more hot air than the cows in his barn, but when her morning run accidentally triggers a stampede across his front yard, Ria's forced to admit the rancher may have a point.

Except she isn't ready to give up her shot at getting out of debt, and if Ria wants a fighting chance to prove herself, she has to figure out why the accidents on the ranch are beginning to feel a lot less — well — accidental.

Untitled Contemporary Romance Thriller

Elena Reynolds is Boston's darling. The city can't get enough of the prima ballerina who cheered for the Red Sox and fried up cannoli at her family's restaurant before leaving home to study under one of Austria's most respected ballet masters.

Now she's home, pirouetting for local morning shows and smiling from billboards as the season ramps up. But behind her poised smiles and graceful ronds de jambes, Elena is afraid. The skin at the back of her neck prickles as she walks home from rehearsal and she knows the darkness she hoped to leave behind has followed her across the Atlantic.
